## Should you worry if your child is an outcast at school?

No, this might actually be a good sign. School is for average kids. They are the ones who feel comfortable there. Those who feel uncomfortable at school are the ones who grow up to be interesting people.

> I know you feel alone and that you don't really belong. You've always felt different, and kids are not very kind to those who don't fit in. You never have. Don't worry, this will be your superpower. You don't know it now, but those who don't fit in end up being the ones who stand out.
