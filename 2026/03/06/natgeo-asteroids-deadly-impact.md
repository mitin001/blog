# [Asteroids: Deadly Impact](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8VgO1adlEM8)

Footage from this film appears in multiple episodes of [Необъяснимо, но факт](../../../2025/10/27/nnf.md).

## How does the *National Geographic* documentary *Asteroids: Deadly Impact* describe what it depicts?

More hit than miss. Some of the footage is described correctly and precisely (e.g., the 1972 Grand Teton Meteor is not claimed to be an alien ship like it is in *Необъяснимо, но факт*), but there are several instances of style over substance, and it even uses real footage to carry out a simulation of a hypothetical scenario (e.g., we see an early Apollo rocket act the part of the Energiya rocket on its way to deflect an Earth-crossing asteroid).
