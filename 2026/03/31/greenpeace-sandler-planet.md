## How does the video *Молочные братья - Убивать планету нельзя* describe what it depicts?

With lyrics from the song. The striking visuals support its message: that we're killing the planet. The footage of our civilization's abuse of nature is interleaved with images of protesters going to shocking lengths to get the attention of the public to their cause. Some wear gas masks, others line the streets with their bodies scattered and accessorized in such a way we are to presume they are dead.
