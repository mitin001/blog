## How to addict someone to something? 

Use positive reinforcement, but not every time. Learn from app developers.

> They learned how to use intermittent rewards, variable ratio reward schedules, to hook people.

We're addicted to our phones because they promise to reward us with jolts of dopamine, but when it comes to delivering on that promise, they're inconsistent. As a result, we keep checking our phones—frantically—because the unpredictability of when the reward will come keeps us focused on them. The element of surprise amplifies the pleasure.
