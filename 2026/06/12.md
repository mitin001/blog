## Bill Maher's definition of an AI chatbot

> A lobotomy with a monthly fee.

[Real Time with Bill Maher: 6/6/2026](https://podcastindex.org/podcast/742515?episode=55844461357), [transcribed](https://writecomments.com/transcripts/?md5=18c8dbaa06e31ab8ee7ec397f3d93765).

You can't teach anyone who won't learn. This is why if someone asks you to show them how to do something, you can't do it for them. You have to watch them do it themselves. As soon as you start doing it for them, they'll forget how to do it themselves. This is why we can't outsource to AI things that we could do just as well without it. Why spend the precious tokens drafting an overly officious sounding letter when you could do a better job writing just off the top of your head? Don't let AI turn you into an infant who cries it for their parent at the sight of anything that could present them with the slightest inconvenience. Use it to give you an edge, not replace your brain.
