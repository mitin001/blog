## Alcohol consumption

> A leading modifiable cancer risk factor.

[Alcohol and Cancer Risk: The U.S. Surgeon General’s Advisory 2025].

If you are willing to commit to only one behavioral change but still want to meaningfully reduce your lifetime cancer risk, start avoiding alcohol. This alone will put you ahead of the curve on health.
