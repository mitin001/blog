This TEDx talk was referenced by [Algospeak](../../../2025/11/20/algospeak.md). It defined _redpilled_.

## What does it mean to be redpilled?

The suffix -pilled means letting something become your whole personality, being overtaken by a fad.

> Convinced into a lifestyle.

In _redpilled_, _red_ refers to Republican (_blue_ would be Democratic). That is, being redpilled means being convinced to become a Republican.
