## What is masculinity?

Masculinity is protecting the people around you. It's generating surplus value for your community rather than being a drain on it. It's doing things for others in a way that's not self-serving. This is the true test of masculinity.

> Ask yourself a question, _are you optimizing for attention or for service?_

When you are helping somebody, are you doing it to show how good of a person you are or how smart you are, or are you doing it to be of service to others? In other words, are you doing it for yourself or for the benefit of society? When you're gone, society's still going to be here. You may only live on in the minds of the people that you've helped. Don't be remembered for being a jerk. If you feel like that's where you're headed with your legacy, it's not too late to stop. Just ask Scott Galloway.

> If someone was rude to me or coming off in traffic, I had to restore equilibrium to the universe and get back in their face.

When you embody masculinity, you don't have the time to attack others over your hurt feelings. You're a soldier, loyal to the cause you believe will benefit society.

> I got a job to do. I'm in service here.
