## How are Kalshi and Polymarket putting the American national security at risk?

Because they significantly cripple the ability to launch surprise attacks on American adversaries, argues Elissa Slotkin. Someone with the knowledge of the impending attack isn't prohibited from making money off this knowledge by placing a bet on it. Insider trading laws don't yet apply to betting platforms.

> A complete operational security risk. If you're the Iranians and you're sitting there and you're like, _huh, I noticed there's a bunch of bets coming in for a war tomorrow_. It's like, _oh shit, I think we're about to have a war_.
