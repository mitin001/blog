# [Indoor Humidity Estimate](https://chatgpt.com/s/t_69dc32462c0c819190aa8baa69a77f4e)

This conversation with [ChatGPT](../../../2025/12/28/chatbots.md) answered a question about controlling indoor mold through controlling relative indoor humidity.

## When can I open the windows in my house if I don't want it to grow mold? 

When the outdoor dew point is 15-20°F lower than the indoor temperature.

> 80°F → ~60°F dew point. 70°F → ~50°F dew point. 62°F → ~42–44°F dew point. That lines up very well with safe indoor humidity (~45–50% RH). One small refinement. Subtract 20°F → keeps you around ~50% RH (ideal). Subtract 15°F → ~60% RH (upper safe limit).
