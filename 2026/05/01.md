## Your toy bunny may be watching you.

> With a few clicks, this popular home assistant toy was turned into a surveillance camera.

[Ctrl + Alt + Chaos].

The proliferation of smart devices a cybersecurity nightmare. Any Internet-connected device with a sensor becomes a surveillance device once a hacker finds a way to intercept the data it transmits. If the sensor is a camera, the hacker has their eyes into your house.

## How long before black hats learn to crash cars through remote code execution?

> Hackers showed off a new technique they’d invented to control a car’s steering wheel, brakes and fuel gauge from a laptop.

[Ctrl + Alt + Chaos].

The world saw a demo of a car control takeover at DEF CON. So the battle between black hats and cybersecurity experts is now on track to overtake yet another new playing field: vehicles zipping through our streets and freeways.
