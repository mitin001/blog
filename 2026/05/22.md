## The American aversion to taking time off

> I work 9-5 in an office. Your shop is open from 9-5. I will never be able to go to your shop. Why did we design our society like this?

[Threads](https://www.threads.com/@shady_et/post/C3uzPNTNG0h).

But we didn't design our society like this. At least, we didn't design all of society like this. It's just the United States that's like this. In the rest of the world, people get guaranteed paid vacation time, so there's far more traffic in your shop during business hours if your ship is not in America.
