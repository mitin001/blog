## Greed runs the world

> Making money is good. Wanting to be rich is good. That's part of what makes America America. We conform to human nature, which is greedy.

[Real Time with Bill Maher: 5/9/2026](https://podcastindex.org/podcast/742515?episode=54429138571), [transcribed](https://writecomments.com/transcripts/?md5=6b2228ffd1eb65fe4d5a01042738d1b8).

The realization that greed runs the world may be an indictment of the human race, but it shouldn't be. The fact that capitalism has become so successful is simply a confirmation that this is how the collective human organism is meant to function. If you want your system to succeed (economic or otherwise), make it an extension of what already comes naturally to the human being. Greed is motivating. Intermittent rewards are addicting. Habits deliver results. It's all about finding the direction of the current underlying human behavior and having it take you where you need to be. It's never about fighting it. You can't go against the flow if you want to get anywhere.
