## Your AI assistant is not your friend. It is a subscription service.

> However much an app might sound like a friend, what it is is a machine, and behind that machine is a corporation trying to extract a monthly fee from you.

[Last Week Tonight](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_Last_Week_Tonight_with_John_Oliver_episodes): April 26, 2026, [transcribed](https://writecomments.com/transcripts/?md5=9a75922c14d3029a42c60057c7792ca6).

AI chatbots are in stage one of enshittification. They're good to users as they're trying to get them addicted to their conversations. In the next stage, the priority will shift to their business customers, and in the one after that, the priority will shift away from everyone. That's the trajectory every big Internet business has followed so far.
