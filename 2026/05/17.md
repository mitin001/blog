## Freedom is not free, but let's make it cost money, not lives

> When I see people spouting slogans like "Peace, not war," "Unity, not division," I roll my eyes. Of course this is what we all want. The adult question is, at what price?

[Real Time with Bill Maher: 4/11/2026](https://podcastindex.org/podcast/742515?episode=53294584919), [transcribed](https://writecomments.com/transcripts/?md5=141e231a0c97f32ca36235dbda2e1c4c).

The mature 21st-century approach to world peace is not violence. It is global trade. Get people interested in putting aside their weapons so that they can make money. Peace should be the ultimate benefit of capitalism. Greed is less of a vice than violence.
