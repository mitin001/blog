## What are the sources of background music for *Необъяснимо, но факт: 9. Святые чудеса*?

During an interview with Sergey Bezrukov, "George Jinda - Here to Stay" plays in the background. "We Bow Down Before Your Cross" also makes an appearance on the soundtrack.
