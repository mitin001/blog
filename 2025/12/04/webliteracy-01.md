This chapter of [Web Literacy](../../../2025/12/04/webliteracy.md) answered a question about propaganda in the digital age.

## Why are there more ill-informed people now that access to information has become democratized? 

The Internet has taught us that giving someone access to all the information in the world doesn't make them informed.

The Internet made it easier for bad actors to exploit human vulnerabilities that make them susceptible to propaganda. It's not enough to have access to information. One must learn the skills to filter out the bad information, and when there's a lot of something, most of it is bad.
