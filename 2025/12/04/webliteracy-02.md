## Why should you never hesitate to abandon something that's not working because of sunk costs? 

If you've already invested so much in something, and it's not working, your investment wasn't in vain. You've paid for invaluable knowledge: that something you've tried is not the right way of doing it. When you start over, you might just discover that investing so much of your time in the dead end made you into a person who can now solve this problem. All you need might just be a different approach.
