## Why do the album covers for Lady Gaga's _Mayhem_ show pieces of a broken mirror?

It's not just a broken mirror, it's images of someone (her) deliberately reassembling it, Lady Gaga explains.

This is a metaphor for the album. It is a reconstruction of music from Lady Gaga's earliest albums. They shattered as she was getting overworked and subjected to experiences that left her with psychological trauma. Now that she's in recovery, she's trying to put the pieces back together, and _Mayhem_ is her attempt at that.
