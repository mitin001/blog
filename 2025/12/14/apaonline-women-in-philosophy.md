## How should we speak up for a marginalized community when we're not a part of it?

Carefully and only after giving members of the community the opportunity to review what you want to say.

> There is a risk in hurting marginalized groups when one writes about them from positions of privilege. On the other hand, I don’t want to leave the burden of responding to posts about trans issues to trans people alone. So, I’ve decided to post this after receiving very helpful feedback from several trans scholars and trans allies.
