## How to counter cynicism? 

Show people they can trust others. This can be taught. The skill someone must learn to start seeing others as trustworthy or not is fact-checking.

> Fact-checking as “tools for trust”, a guard against the cynicism that cognitive overload often produces.

Countering cynicism is important because a cynical society is particularly vulnerable to an authoritarian takeover. Once people have given up on the truth or decided that the truth is unknowable, they embrace a strongman and allow him to dictate to them what to believe.
