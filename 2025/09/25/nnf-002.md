Like any other episode of [Необъяснимо, но факт](../../../2025/10/27/nnf.md), this film is a collection of fragments of other films mixed with original footage.

## What are the sources of background music for *Необъяснимо, но факт: 2. Кыштымский пришелец*?

Much of the same music as [*Необъяснимо, но факт: 1. В объятиях смерти*](../2025/09/27/nnf-001.md), however the makers dipped into some new production music albums to keep the sound fresh:
* [ATMOS 28] - a new album, the first volume of [ATMOS 55], which was used previously
* [ATMOS 149] - another new album from the ATMOS library
* [NJJ 54] - a new album from the [NJJ library](https://productionmusic.fandom.com/wiki/New_York_Beats); both ATMOS and NJJ are distributed by Universal Production Music
* [CDM 50] - a new album from the CDM library; a track from [CDM 54] was used previously
* [CDM 61] - another new album from the CDM library
* [KOS 24] - a new album from the [KOS library](https://productionmusic.fandom.com/wiki/Kosinus), related to the [KOKA library], which was used previously
* [DWCD 0230]

The makers also used some new tracks from the previously used production music albums (e.g., track 43 of [ATMOS 55] was used in the previous episode, and tracks 20 and 73 were used in this one). Two tracks of the soundtrack album [Joseph LoDuca – Brotherhood Of The Wolf] made it into this episode. The episode also embedded fragments of an episode of another television series, which used [Vangelis - Thermo Vision], tracks from the soundtrack album [Peter Gabriel – Passion], and a number of unidentified tracks.
