Like any other episode of [Необъяснимо, но факт](../../../2025/10/27/nnf.md), this film is a collection of fragments of other films mixed with original footage. I tried using the reverse image search engines Google, Yandex, and TinEye on a set of frames extracted from the film to tell which footage was original and which was sourced from elsewhere.
