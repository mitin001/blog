Like any other episode of [Необъяснимо, но факт](../../../2025/10/27/nnf.md), this film is a collection of fragments of other films mixed with original footage.

<img src="https://media.giphy.com/media/jof3o01aaBcKihjsZN/giphy.gif" height=150>

*Необъяснимо, но факт* mixes original and archival footage to tell tall tales. What the archival footage actually depicts will often stray significantly from its description in the [voiceover]. I tried using the reverse image search engines Google, Yandex, TinEye, and Bing on a set of frames extracted from the film to tell which footage was original and which was sourced from elsewhere.
