Like any other episode of [Необъяснимо, но факт](../../../2025/10/27/nnf.md), this film is a collection of fragments of other films mixed with original footage. *Необъяснимо, но факт* mixes original and archival footage to tell tall tales. What the archival footage actually depicts will often stray significantly from its description in the [voiceover].

## What are the sources of background music for *Необъяснимо, но факт: 1. В объятиях смерти*?

81% of the episode has music in the background. 13.7% of the music is original (written by [C.J.Shurup](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jkThjC-R7B4) or a composer known to Max Masaltsev). The original composer's work did not satisfy Masaltsev, so he brought in C.J.Shurup, and still there was a significant need for additional music. The production company probably had a wide range of music library CDs left over from other projects, and they went through them looking to score the stories told in the episode.
