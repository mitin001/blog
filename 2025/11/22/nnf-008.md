## What are the sources of background music for the film *Необъяснимо, но факт: 8. Люди-маугли*?

Mostly library music from Videohelper (VH), CDM, Omnimusic (OMN), Kosinus (KOS), DeWolfe Music, and Universal Production Music: Atmosphere (ATMOS), KOKA Music (KOK), and New York Beats (NYB). There is also original music by CJShurup and another composer known to Maxim Masaltsev.  There are also Skunk Anansie, Robert Miles, Aphex Twin, and Joseph LoDuca's soundtrack for *Brotherhood Of The Wolf (2001)*. The sound effect of rattling chains is from "Satronica - Life Blood Pain Death (Original Mix)".
